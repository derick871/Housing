# Global-Energies
## Slowing Growth: 
Global energy demand increased by 1.3% in 2025 (approximately 8 exajoules), which is a deceleration from the rapid 2% growth in 2024.
## Renewable Energy Surge: 
Solar PV was the largest contributor to demand growth in 2025, for the first time on record for a modern renewable source.
## Fuel Mix:
 While low-emissions sources dominated growth, natural gas and oil also saw demand increases in 2025, while coal demand growth slowed, driven by declines in China and India.2026 Emergency Status: A shutdown of the Strait of Hormuz due to war has severely affected global energy security in 2026, leading to calls for reduced fuel use in major economies like India.
