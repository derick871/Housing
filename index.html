Executive Overview

This report outlines the shift toward sustainable power grids in 2025. As technology matures, efficiency rates have climbed, making renewable sources more competitive with traditional fossil fuels.

Key Takeaways

  • Solar remains the fastest-growing sector globally.
  • Geothermal provides the highest base-load efficiency.
  • Infrastructure investment increased by 12% year-over-year.
